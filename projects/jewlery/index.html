Jewelry & Metal Casting

Practical home casting: simple tools, controlled heat, clean pours, and usable results. This page will expand with patterns, molds, and settings work.

Completed work

  • Aluminum test coupons to validate mold flow and surface finish.
  • Cast aluminum ring prototypes to prove workflow before precious metals.
  • Chain mail project (assembled links, finishing and fit-up).

Process overview

Pattern → Mold prep → Preheat → Melt & degas → Pour → Shakeout → Cut/Grind → Finish/Polish
StageNotes
Furnace/heatHome-built charcoal kiln verified for Al; temp measured, safe handling PPE.
MoldsSimple sand molds for rings and coupons; working toward multi-part patterns.
AlloysAluminum scrap for practice; precious metals planned once fit/finish are reliable.
FinishingSawing, deburring, file work, sanding grits, polish; ring sizing tests ongoing.

Tools & materials

  • Charcoal kiln, crucible, tongs, IR thermometer, fire-safe workspace.
  • Basic bench tools: vise, files, saws, sandpaper, polishing compound.
  • Metrology: calipers, ring sizer, simple gauges.
