Audio Visualizer

A fully analog multi-channel visualizer: five frequency bands, ten amplitude levels, no digital processing. This page will document the circuit, filters, power design, and enclosure once construction is complete.

Status

  • Concept proven on breadboard — individual band-pass filters respond correctly.
  • Next step: PCB layout and enclosure design.
  • Target: modular analog bars driven by LM3915 or discrete comparator ladders.

Planned features

  • Five fixed frequency bands spanning 63 Hz → 8 kHz.
  • Adjustable input gain, per-band response shaping, and color lighting.
  • All-analog processing path — no microcontroller required.
  • External 12 V DC input with isolated logic and lighting rails.
